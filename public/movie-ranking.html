The Best Movies Ever

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Singin' In The Rain

With its perfectly balanced mixture of singing, dancing, comedy, and romance, Singin' in the Rain is now widely regarded as the greatest film musical ever made.

Forrest Gump

The film, "Forrest Gump", deals with finding the true meaning of the life despite facing numerous hurdles. It encourages never to stop and cry over the past experiences. The story is about a man named Forrest Gump. He was born with disabled legs and had a very low IQ of 75.

The Devil Wears Prada

In the glamorous world of fashion journalism, "The Devil Wears Prada" paints a captivating picture of unwavering ambition, pursuing lifelong dreams, and the harsh realities of a toxic workplace. Starring Meryl Streep and Anne Hathaway, this film offers intriguing recruitment lessons that resonate in any industry.
